# Apps

## [ZenSpan](https://zenspan.app)

My interpretation for what a meditation timer on my phone should be. You can set a duration, set when interval sounds should play and that's about it. The interval sound is a singing bowl to keep your focus on the meditation. To keep it from being monotonous, it plays a different recording every time. After the meditation, your session is logged with an emoji, which you can also see in a widget.

- Platform: iOS
- Website: [zenspan.app](https://zenspan.app)

## [PeekCard](https://peekcard.app)

I wanted an app that could store QR codes and barcodes, but not do much else. The most important part for me was that I could put a QR code or barcode in a widget, to make it easily accessible from my iPhone home screen.

- Platform: iOS
- Website: [peekcard.app](https://peekcard.app)

## [theshook.one](https://theshook.one)

This started out as a webpage to show a minimal view of the articles from theverge.com. But since then it has progressed to showing content from [Daring Fireball](https://daringfireball.net/), [Macrumors](https://www.macrumors.com/) and this website.

Still a very minimal layout and shows which links are new since your last visit. I open this one quite regularly, but have no idea who else is actually visiting this site.

- Platform: www
- Website: [theshook.one](https://theshook.one)
